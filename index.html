How It Works

A clear, collaborative process with no surprises.

  1. 01

    Discovery Call

    We learn your requirements, constraints, and timeline. You get a fixed-price quote within 24 hours.

  2. 02

    Schematic Design

    Clean, hierarchical schematics delivered for your review. We iterate until you’re happy before touching the layout.

  3. 03

    PCB Layout

    Optimised placement and routing with SI / PI considerations baked in. Progress updates every two days.

  4. 04

    DFM Review

    Automated DRC plus manual review against your chosen fab’s capabilities. Issues fixed before delivery.

  5. 05

    Gerber Delivery

    Full production package handed off. We stay available for fab queries during manufacturing.
